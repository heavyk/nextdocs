Books About Paranormal

True Ghosts Stories

by Hans Holzer

Hans Holzer made a career out of exploring the paranormal. This book is filled with cases that show just how powerful the invisible world can be; how what we can't see, and in the case of some people what they don't believe exists, CAN reach out and literally touch us.


True Ghost Stories

Ghost Ships

by Richard Winer

An interesting book of paranormal phenomena associated with the sea.


Ghost Ships: True Stories of Nautical Nightmares, Hauntings, and Disasters

Hidden Files: Law Enforcement's True Case Stories of the Unexplained and Paranormal

by Sue Kovach

This book deals with paranormal cases encountered by law enforcement professionals. Information about these sorts of cases usually never leave the office probably due to ridicule. So, this book is a rareity in that the author got a number of LE professionals to talk about some of these paranormal cases.


Hidden Files: Law Enforcement's True Case Stories of the Unexplained and Paranormal

Adobe Angels: The Ghosts of Las Cruces & Southern New Mexico

by Antonio R. Garcez

This is the third book in the series of New Mexico ghosts. The author has interviewed people with ghost experiences and let them tell their story. The stories are well presented and represent the entire range of paranormal phenomena. Each story makes you want to read the next. Special feature: book is Bilingual- English/Spanish
