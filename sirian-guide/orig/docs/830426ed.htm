AN INSPIRATIONAL MESSAGE FROM SIR ARTHUR CONAN DOYLE

The Path of Spiritual Growth

Presented April 26, 1983

Channeled by George

I Bid you a good evening.

I would like to commend you for the progress that has been made up to this point. It would appear that the opposition is quite displeased, and they have been putting a great deal of pressure on some of the members of the circle. Each of you have your weak spots, so to speak. 'Tis best that you be consciously aware of them so that they can't be used against you. It seems appropriate at this point to cite an old cliche, that is; that it's always darkest before the dawn.

We began this work a long time ago. We've all spent a great deal of earth time putting each brick in place to build the structure. It means so much to all of us here and to each of you; each in your own way. All have various roles to perform. Some will make it, some won't; but the choice is yours. To cite another cliche, they say the path of righteousness is narrow. 'Tis true, because it's harder to climb uphill than it is to slide downhill. 'Tis easy to fall, many of us have. But we have the choice of getting up again, carrying on. As I mentioned before, tis important for each of you to look into the past. It could save you many scratches and bruises on the downward slide. Take heart. We have entered into a phase that's been long in the making, and it's new -- very new. Even we here, have to grapple for new ways to accomplish the purposes, as time ticks away.

Now I would like to say that the solution to resolving the difficulties that presently exist lies in team work and team effort; the combination of energies. Combine them, focus them to the points that need to be directed and they will find a receptive atmosphere. There are those of you here who will not understand these words but those of you here that will, that is to whom it's directed. Carry on, continue, strive to evolve in a move upwards. It's by far the shorter path in the long run.

I Bid You A Good Evening.
