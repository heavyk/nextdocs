LESSON 24

DISCOVER THE UNIVERSE

This material is a follow-up from the material given out last week entitled LOVE, although this portion was written months before the other.

Please note that this portion is written mostly in the FIRST RAY style of short, but often paradoxical maxims.

Also, we want to point out that this article has within it the philosophy of the NEW AGE psychology, religion, science, education and ethics. The choices are yours!

The whole of the Universe is within you; just as surely as you are within it.

That which you do for yourself, as you are doing it, you do it to every entity within the Universe and to the Universe itself.

That which you do for others is being done to you as you perform the act for others.

A BRIEF RESTATEMENT

What you do for yourself is being done by you to all others and conversely.

What you do for others is being done back to you by all others.

No one can perform an act affecting himself alone.

The WHOLE is a beautiful geometric pattern with each point connected to all other points by so many rays that the whole admits to no individuals.

An individual is merely a point within ITS consciousness that has been given a specialized task, or responsibility. Within that points' responsibilities/ lives, he has autonomy of choices - complete freedom! The WHOLE does not intrude to discipline or counsel for there is no differentiation to it.

The point may not be aware of the WHOLE, but the Whole is very aware of all of itself.

Ones main responsibility is to see that you become the BEST reflection of YOUR SELF which is the WHOLE.

As a FREE living specialized unit of that ONE, our choices should be carefully considered, for as such, what we choose for ourselves, we have chosen for the WHOLE!

Such freedom brings awesome responsibility, for when I make a choice or take an action, I have done it for and to everyone in the Universe.

To Rephrase: One is free to choose as he decides but in his deciding, he decides for ALL!

For you are ALL, unknowingly. Thus, every individual should be afforded their method of "SELF REALIZATION" for what he IS, is also where he's going. The beginnings and the ends are the same!

By now you probably have realized why we have given the finding of YOURSELF such priority. An entity, upon meeting himself, has found the WHOLE; the UNIVERSE; the ALL - that ONE!

A person, being a specialized extension or RAY of the WHOLE has merely to follow himself back to that WHOLE. Therefore, you are your path. You have already been where you are going!

In seeking Self, you seek the WHOLE.

In finding SELF, you have found the Whole.

In loving SELF, you love the whole.

BUT, to KNOW SELF, IS TO BE.... a god.
