LESSON 19

ATMIC

The ATMIC plane is the third plane and as such, is the receiver of the third aspects energy from without this system. This is the first plane we have studied that represents an Aspect of Divinity.

The vehicle there, for the MONAD, is analogous to the physical for the SOUL, and yet it is the antithesis of physical existence. Where the physical is limited to one location at a time, the ATMIC realizes omnipresence; where the physical thinks linearly, the ATMIC uses circular thought whose center is everywhere and circumference is nowhere.

This is the plane of Nirvana. The above explanation of the differences of physical and ATMIC reality is why some have referred to it as annihilation. The ATMIC, being the lowest plane that FIRST RAY energy from outside the Solar System can penetrate, is where both ends of paradoxes are equally true. The entity is equally aware of individuality, as he is of oneness with all there is.

The ATMIC is a wondrous dynamic plane. The MONAD sends into the ATMIC vehicle its FIRST RAY energy activating its purpose and executing its will from that point.

Upon this plane man becomes a god among gods.
