LESSON 21

MONADIC PLANE

The MONADIC plane is the second plane from the top and is the repository for all life essences within this system. This is due to the fact that this Solar System is perfecting the Second Aspect.

This plane is also the receiver of all SECOND RAY energy sent from the Cosmic ASTRAL plane. Its color is indigo; blue so dark that it can be compared to the sky at night.

From the MONADIC radiates the Second Aspect or ray into all other planes of this system. The fire of life burns there and also radiates downward. It is the home of the HIGHER SELF and is the place that humans are struggling to return to through evolution.

Upon the MONADIC plane, is the Monadic Capsule. In order for the unmanifested power to manifest, it must ray forth The ONE. The ONE is manifested power but incapable of action so it demands the Third. The Second becomes necessary to relate the powers to their creations. Therefore, creators in manifestation all have three aspects. The human MONAD is no exception.

Upon the MONADIC plane, your Higher Self resides as a CREATOR replete with its three aspects. If your MONAD is THIRD RAY then your aspects are the FIRST, SECOND, and THIRD RAYS of the Cosmic Third Ray.

If you are ALIEN to this system, then somewhere in the order of things resides your Higher Self, although it would have its representative on some level in this system. Some ALIENS require mechanisms to step down their vibrations so that they can exist on Earth.
