INTUITION

Intuition - the process of knowing without ratiocination - is a faculty developed by the functioning of the connection to the BUDDHIC vehicle.

Much more precise information can be gathered this way than by depending on the ASTRAL record reading or feeling. Things upon the ASTRAL are a reversal of the BUDDHIC. Here is a hint as to the problem people have with reversals; the connections between the Spiritual Triad have not become completely dominant over the physical faculties and the CONCRETE MIND confuses the two sets of impulses. Discrimination has to be taught to the CONCRETE MIND so that it can render unto the physical that which is physical and unto the spiritual that which is of its kind. If you are interested, we could give you some helpful suggestions for adults and children with the problem of reversals.

The BUDDHIC plane faculty of intuition is one that is easier for even rays. There is another faculty that stems from the ATMIC plane that odd rays find easier to deal with in obtaining "knowing". This will be discussed when we talk about the that plane.

Later in the course, we'll give processes for the development of intuitional functioning. For now, it is sufficient for you to be aware that it is a faculty that you will develop and to be on the look out for it.

The Sirian Cortege

EXERCISE FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE LIGHT VEHICLE

To practice this exercise, orient your feet towards the approximate location of Sirius which can be determined with a star chart wheel.

1. Process: lay on the floor, or a bed if you can orient in the correction direction, which is toward Sirius. Establish a slow, deep breathing, from the abdomen. Concentrate your attention on the abdomen and breathing with it. Maintain this throughout the entire process.

2. As you continue the deep breathing from the abdomen, you will notice a lightness or tingling sensation at the base of the spine. Soon your body will lighten up from your head to your feet.

3. Begin to visualize this lightness as a luminance; the light vehicle. By will, begin exuding the light vehicle out all around you for about two inches; as if to alleviate the effects of gravity.

4. When you have felt the lightness and the Light vehicle extends approximately two inches from your physical body, tell it to stand up in front of you and see it doing so in your mind's eye.

5. Contemplate the Light vehicle; examine all its details. Notice how its separation from your physical body affects the physical body.

6. When you have completed your examination, tell the Light vehicle to merge back into your physical body. Feel it resuming it normal orientation within the body.

7. Notice the difference with the re-entry of the Light vehicle.

8. Return to the normal levels of conscious.
