Spiritualism

The Spiritualist movement began in 1848 when the Fox sisters began to receive messages from a spirit who said he was murdered by a former tenet in the house that their family rented . Communication with spirits took the nation by storm and crossed the Atlantic to Great Britain.

Spiritualism was a great step forward in making people aware of the spirit world and spiritual thought. The movement exists in the present. This section will present information on Spiritualism topics.

Tura Britt Stone, Physical Medium by Robert M. Stone, M.S.
