Spiritualism News Reports of the Past

There was a time when Spiritualism was newsworthy with many educated and distinguished people presenting their experiences with the reality of life after death. This section is to preserve those news treasures. The reader is encouraged to go to the library and explore the original articles. Also, read about the people mentioned in the articles. Ironically even those scientists and authors, such as Sir Oliver Lodge, to whom spiritualism was just as valid as science, are only remembered today for their science not their extensive work with spiritualism. It's time to set the historical record straight.
