UFO BOOKS - CONTACT AND ABDUCTIONS

Finding good UFO books is difficult because the so-called debunkers title their works deceptively to lure the UFO novice. We have read the following books have found them to contain useful information to aid in learning about peoples experiences with UFOs and extra-terrestrials..

 

Witness to Roswell

by Thomas J. Carey, Donald R. Schmitt

 This book offers conclusive evidence that an extra-terrestrial spacecraft crashed in New Mexico in 1947 and alien bodies were recovered.  

Witnessed

by Budd Hopkins

Written by the pioneer of alien abduction research, this books tells the story of a New York City abduction with some very high profile witnesses. Worth reading but out of print.


     

Left at East Gate

by Larry Warren and Peter Robbins

This interesting book details the 1980 UFO incident in England involving a nuclear ready NATO base manned by U.S. Air Force personnel.


Abducted!

by Debbie Jordan & Kathy Mitchell

This book tells the story of a family's encounter with grey aliens. I've met Debbie Jordan. She is a strong person who has endured a great deal.



Available Used from Amazon

The Truth About the UFO Crash at Roswell

by Kevin D. Randle & Donald R. Schmitt

There have been a number of books on Roswell. I found this title at the UFO Museam in Roswell, NM. Although currently out of print, there should be some paperbacks available. Although a good book, Witness to Roswell IS the final chapter and wraps up the story.

 

The Allagash Abductions

By Raymond E. Fowler

This is an excellent book that documents the exploration of a group abduction by extra-terrestrials in 1976. If you want to know how an alien abduction is explored by investigators, this is the book for you.
